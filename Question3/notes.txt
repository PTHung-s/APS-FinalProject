Question 3 audio data notes

Raw recording:
- Device/microphone: Behringer XM8500 microphone.
- Raw format: MKV recordings.
- Recorded sampling rate: 48 kHz.
- Raw channel setting: stereo.

Processed analysis files:
- Audio was extracted from the MKV files using FFmpeg.
- The extracted WAV files were converted to mono, 16-bit PCM.
- All clips were trimmed to the first 9 seconds.
- Final analysis sampling rate: 48 kHz.
- Final clip length: 9 seconds, or 432000 samples per clip.

Data set:
- Ambient noise class: 10 clips of room/background noise.
- Music class: 10 clips of music played from a phone approximately 1 cm away from the microphone.
- The same microphone, sampling rate, conversion process, and clip length were used for both classes.

Entropy settings:
- Audio amplitudes were read as normalized samples in the range [-1, 1].
- Bin range: [-1, 1].
- Bin width: Delta = 0.1.
- Number of bins: K = 20.
